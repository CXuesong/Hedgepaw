The Cave

You enter the cave and see many large dragons scattered about, some with hatchlings, sleeping on piles of gold.

You also see a pile of scrolls on the ground. Having been here before, you have started recording information about the growth and progress of your eggs.

Near the entrance to the cave, there are some abandoned eggs. If you don’t want the dragons inside to die, you can take one of those instead.

One of the scrolls in the pile contains a map charting dragon habitats across Galsreim and the surrounding area. Where would you like to travel?
